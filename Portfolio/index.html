What Moves Me

my flow

Movement


Movement creates a natural dynamism that inspires each of my pieces. Across mediums, I am inspired by the rhythm shared in life; of body movement, the lapse of time, and the countless hours it takes to master a craft. In documentaries, the height of a story can result from a flash of movement or fast compilation of clips. Each shift adds a beat and functionality that is undeniably captivating.


Sound


Evocative work is softened by sound; the hum of a bird, an orchestral score, or the voiceover in a narrative brings a wealth of information to me as a research and explore. To understand more about the world around me, I listen and seek to know more about the evolutionary landscape of our world.



Remix


The world is constantly changing, the earth is evolving around new discoveries are made each day. Comparing truths from the past and present allow me to create work that questions the future. Through the use of interactive design, I seek to contribute to groundbreaking explorations informed by what we know and projecting future information.
